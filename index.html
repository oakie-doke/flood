Information

How to Use FloodSafe

1. Enter your city, zip code, address or press "Use Current Location."
2. FloodSafe will use rainfall, elevation, and geographical data to calculate a Risk Score between 1 and 100.
3. If the area is deemed high risk for flooding, FloodSafe will find the closest nearby safe city.
4. Click "Get Directions" to be redirected to Google Maps and find your route to safety.

How It Works

Location Assessment

Enter your address or use your current location to get started. FloodSafe fetches real-time elevation and weather data for your location from US Geological Survey, Open Elevation API, and National Weather Service.

Risk Calculation

Our algorithm considers multiple factors including elevation, recent rainfall, and proximity to water bodies to calculate your current flood risk level (Low, Medium, High, or Very High).

Emergency Planning

If your area is at risk, FloodSafe will find the closest safe city and redirect you to Google Maps.
